About us...

It all began in the year 1987, when two visionaries met, in a very humble way. Shri Jitendra N. Shah, from the traditional Gujrati Trading Community, Shri Prabhulal R. Joshi, from the Brahmin community of Menar, in Rajasthan and Shri Manoj N Shah. They all had strong business acumen, and the later had extensive experience, in the tourism and catering industry. Together they floated Explore Tours & Travels, in Vile Parle, Mumbai, and started operating from a rented office of just 75 sq. Ft. They started by operating fixed departure tours to the Bangalore – Mysore – Ooty sector, with a unique feature of an accompanying vegetarian, catering team.

After the considerable success of the new venture, over the years they expanded their foot print, to cover all the major tourist circuits in India. To their credit they have, the distinction of being the pioneers, of operating fixed departure, group tours, exclusively for Honeymooners. These special Honeymoon packages, meticulously planned and perfectly executed, were a unique proposition. of aromatic atmosphere, cause all your co – travellers, are also on their Honeymoon, along with all the other benefits, of a group tour. This prompted, the famous Gujrati Magazine Chitralekha to, come out with a cover story on this pioneering venture in the year 1989.

Today Explore Tours & Travels operates from its own 1000 sq. ft. 5 offices just in Vile Parla out of which one is the Head Office. 17 Sales offices, in key Tourist Markets of Bangalore, Ahmedabad, Surat, Rajkot, Pune, Chennai, Bhavnagar, Jamnagar, Vadodara, Hyderabad, Aurangabad, Jodhpur, Surendranagar, Dahod, Bhuj, Kolhapur and Nasik . Heena tours operates fixed departure Brochure tours on a weekly basis, to all the important Tourist circuits like Rajasthan, Himachal, Kashmir, Uttaranchal, Kumaon, Sikkim, Kerala, Karnataka-Tamilnadu, with their vegetarian catering team accompanying all the tours. “Explore Tours & Travels” has 300 personals, on its rolls, consisting of Tour managers, Escorts, Catering staff, and Sales team.
